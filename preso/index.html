Kubernetes Architecture and Components

Kubernetes is an orchestration platform for running containers.

Terminology

  • Namespace
  • Container
  • Pod
  • Replication Controller
  • Service
  • ConfigMap
  • Secret
  • Volume

Namespaces

Namespaces are the highest level of organization in Kubernetes.

Containers and Pods

Containers are the running images.
Pods are the smallest unit of scalability within Kubernetes.

Replication Controller

The Replication Controller is in charge of managing the running of the pods.
